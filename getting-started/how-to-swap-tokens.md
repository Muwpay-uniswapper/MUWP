# How to Swap Tokens

{% hint style="info" %}
Open the dApp by clicking on the link above in the navigation bar
{% endhint %}

Start by connecting your wallet with MUWP

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/CleanShot 2024-01-22 at 21.54.17@2x.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>Hover the swap card with your cursor to make the "Connect" button appear</p></figcaption></figure>

2. Select the chain where your tokens are, and select which tokens you want to swap

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/CleanShot 2024-01-22 at 21.58.09@2x.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>You can select any token to get a quote, including the ones you don't own</p></figcaption></figure>

3. Select your desired output chain & token

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/CleanShot 2024-01-22 at 22.00.23@2x.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>You can also choose to stay on the same chain</p></figcaption></figure>

4. Click on "Find Routes" to get a quote
5. Visualise the path and select different routes

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/CleanShot 2024-01-22 at 22.02.43@2x.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>Click on show all routes when hovering the input to choose a different route</p></figcaption></figure>

6. Approve and swap

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Capture d’écran 2024-01-22 à 22.04.36.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>Just follow the instructions on screen!</p></figcaption></figure>

7. Wait for your transaction to finalise!
